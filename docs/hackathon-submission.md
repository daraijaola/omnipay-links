# Hackathon submission copy

## Project name

OmniPay Links

## One-liner

Telegram-native payment links that let merchants accept any supported token and settle in their preferred TON asset through STON.fi Omniston.

## Short description

OmniPay Links helps Telegram creators, freelancers, DAOs, community admins, and small merchants create shareable payment links. The merchant chooses the asset they want to receive, while the payer can pay with the asset they already hold. STON.fi Omniston handles quote discovery, routing, swap execution, and TON settlement behind a simple Telegram Mini App checkout.

## AI tools used

Codex / ChatGPT.

## Integrations used

- STON.fi Omniston SDK/API v1beta8 adapter.
- TON Connect runtime path.
- Telegram Mini App WebApp SDK.
- TonAPI/Toncenter-ready receipt tracking placeholder.
- Vercel static deployment.

## Demo story

Alice is a Telegram creator selling a design service for 50 USDT on TON. Bob wants to pay but only has another supported TON asset. Alice creates an OmniPay Link inside the Telegram Mini App and sends it to Bob. Bob opens the link, connects a wallet, chooses the asset he has, receives an Omniston-powered quote, confirms payment, and Alice receives the settlement asset she requested.

## Winning angle

The next DeFi UX is not a trading terminal. It is normal user actions — pay, subscribe, buy, donate — with STON.fi Omniston hiding liquidity fragmentation in the background.
